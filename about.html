The YourBrand Story

2019

The Problem

After leaking through a $200 pair of yoga pants during a marathon training run, our founder realized something: period products hadn't evolved in 50 years. Tampons were invented in 1931. Pads in 1888. And "period underwear" on the market was either too thin, too synthetic, or too expensive.

2020

The Prototype

Working with textile engineers in Los Angeles, we tested 47 different fabric combinations. The winning formula: 100% organic cotton for the skin-facing layer (because synthetic fabrics against your vulva is a bad idea), a silver-ion antimicrobial treatment, and a breathable PUL leak barrier that doesn't feel like a diaper.

2021

The Launch

We launched with one style (High-Waist) and sold out in 72 hours. 10,000 women joined the waitlist. The reviews were overwhelming: "Why didn't this exist sooner?"

2024

50,000+ Strong

Today, we're the highest-rated period underwear brand in America. 8 styles. XS to 4XL. And a community of women who refuse to compromise on comfort, performance, or values.
