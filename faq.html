How do I wash and care for my period underwear?

Caring for your period underwear is simple and takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Rinse immediately after wear in cold water until the water runs clear
  2. Machine wash on a gentle cycle at 30 deg C (86 deg F) with mild detergent
  3. Hang dry - never tumble dry, as heat can damage the leak-proof layer
  4. Avoid fabric softener and bleach - they reduce absorbency over time

With proper care, your period panties will last 3-5 years (150+ washes). That's 200+ disposable products saved from landfills.
